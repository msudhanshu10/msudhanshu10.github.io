About Me

I am a PhD student at National University of Singapore working in the field of Electrical and Computer Engineering. My research focuses on Surgical AI, Computer-assisted interventions, and Computer vision. I am passionate about developing cutting-edge AI technologies for healthcare applications and aim to contribute to improving surgical outcomes through intelligent systems.

I am starting my PhD journey in January 2025 under the guidance of Prof. Yueming Jin. My background includes experience in computer vision, machine learning, and AI applications in healthcare.

Research

My research focuses on Surgical AI and Computer-assisted interventions. I am particularly interested in computer vision applications in healthcare and their applications to improving surgical outcomes and patient care.

Surgical AI

Developing intelligent systems to assist surgeons in complex procedures, enhancing precision and patient outcomes through AI-driven insights.

Computer-assisted Interventions

Creating innovative tools and technologies that support medical professionals during interventional procedures, improving safety and efficacy.

Computer Vision

Applying advanced computer vision techniques to medical imaging and surgical robotics for better diagnosis and treatment planning.
